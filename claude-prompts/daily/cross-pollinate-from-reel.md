# Convert a reel into a daily tip

Use this prompt when you have a reel script (or a reel transcript) that landed and you want to turn it into an email. Drop into the Claude project that has `CLAUDE.md` loaded.

---

## Prompt

I have a reel script that performed [well / okay / I want to test in email form]. Convert it into a daily tip per `CLAUDE.md`.

**The reel:**

```
[paste reel script or transcript here]
```

**What's different about email vs. reel:**
- Reels are spoken; emails are read. Conversational asides that work in voice often look weird written.
- Reels have a hook in the first 1.5 seconds. Emails have a subject line + 1-sentence opener — same job, different format.
- Reels can lean on visuals/B-roll for context. Emails have to carry it all in words.
- Reels can be 30 seconds. Email tips read in under 60. Both are short, but the email is denser per word.

**What I want from you:**
1. A subject line that captures the same hook the reel opens with.
2. A 1-sentence opener that sets up the same tension.
3. The actual tip in 1-2 sentences (specific action, like in the reel).
4. A 1-sentence action line he can do today.
5. The standard sign-off + P.S. per `CLAUDE.md`.

**Voice notes:**
- If the reel had a sarcastic punchline, keep it. Translate to writing if needed.
- If the reel had a moment that was 100% face-acting / visual gag — drop it, can't be saved.
- The email should feel like the same Chris who's in the reel. Not a tightened-up clinical version.

**Output:**
- The full email in the locked format.
- A one-line note on what you kept, what you cut, and why.

Go.

---

## When this works best

- Reels with a clear single tip ("here's the thing your doctor isn't testing")
- Reels where the punch is verbal, not visual
- Reels you want to extend the lifespan of — turn a 24-hour-organic-reach asset into a permanent record in the email archive

## When to skip this

- Reels that are pure entertainment with no specific takeaway
- Reels that depend on a visual gag (dropping a mic, showing a meme, etc.)
- Reels that are 60+ seconds — those are usually multiple tips. Convert each separately.
