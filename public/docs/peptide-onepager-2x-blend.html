Peptide Protocol Guide
2X GH Blend
A growth hormone secretagogue blend that boosts your body’s natural GH production — improving sleep, body composition, recovery, and energy.
Dose: Provider-determined (1mg–4mg)
Frequency: 5 on / 2 off
Route: Subcutaneous injection
What It Is

CJC-1295 (No DAC)

A growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog. Signals your pituitary to produce more GH with a sustained, extended release.

Ipamorelin

A selective growth hormone-releasing peptide. Triggers a clean GH pulse without raising cortisol, prolactin, or appetite.

Why together? — CJC-1295 extends the GH release window while Ipamorelin amplifies the pulse. Together, they produce a stronger, longer-lasting GH response than either alone.

Benefits
  • Deeper, more restorative sleep
  • Increased lean muscle and strength
  • Accelerated fat loss — especially belly fat
  • Faster workout recovery
  • Improved skin elasticity and hair quality
  • Enhanced energy and mental clarity
  • No cortisol or appetite spikes
When to Use It
  • Low energy or poor sleep quality
  • Stubborn body fat despite diet and exercise
  • Slow recovery from workouts or injuries
  • Age-related decline in muscle and vitality
  • General optimization and longevity
  • Post-injury tissue support
How to Inject
1
Prep. Wash hands. Clean injection site with alcohol pad. Remove syringe from fridge a few minutes early.
2
Inject. Pinch skin at a fatty area (abdomen or thigh). Insert needle at 45° angle. Inject slowly under the skin.
3
Rotate. Use a different injection site each day — alternate between abdomen and thighs.
4
Dispose. Single-use only. Place in sharps container or return to clinic. Never recap or reuse.
Timing

Inject before bed on an empty stomach (2+ hours after eating). GH is released in pulses during deep sleep — evening dosing maximizes this.

What to Expect

Weeks 1 – 2

Better sleep quality and increased energy. Many patients notice deeper, more restorative sleep within the first week.

Months 2 – 3

Visible body composition changes. Fat loss, improved muscle tone, better skin, and sustained energy throughout the day.

Possible Side Effects
  • Mild water retention (usually temporary)
  • Slight redness or swelling at injection site
  • Transient tingling or numbness in hands
  • Occasional headache or fatigue

Usually mild and resolve within the first 1–2 weeks. If symptoms persist, contact us.

Do Not Use If
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Living with active cancer
  • Managing serious liver, kidney, or heart conditions
  • Dealing with uncontrolled medical conditions
  • Allergic to any ingredient
