## Rules of thumb

- **Don't repeat a topic within ~6 months.** A second take from a different angle is fine after that. ("ApoB" is allowed once a year. "Sleep" is allowed every 2-3 weeks if the angle is genuinely different.)
- **Tag aggressively.** Multi-tag tips. Future-you searching for "sleep" will thank you.
- **One topic per day.** If a tip touches two topics (sleep + alcohol), tag both — but don't try to teach two lessons in one tip.
- **The backlog is a working document.** When you have an idea on the way home from the clinic, throw it down here even if it's half-baked. Future-you will tighten it.
