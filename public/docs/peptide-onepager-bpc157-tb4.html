How to Inject
1
Prep. Wash hands. Clean injection site with alcohol pad. Remove syringe from fridge a few minutes early.
2
Inject. Pinch skin at a fatty area (abdomen, thigh, or back of arm). Insert needle at 45° angle. Inject slowly under the skin.
3
Rotate. Use a different injection site each day — alternate between abdomen, thighs, and arms.
4
Dispose. Single-use only. Place in sharps container or return to clinic. Never recap or reuse.
Storage

Keep refrigerated. Inject at the same time each day (morning or evening) for stable peptide levels.

Days 10 – 30

Deeper tissue repair. Consistency compounds — longer use builds more benefit. Strength and flexibility improve.
